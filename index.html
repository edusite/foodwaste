Types of Recycling

Recycling can be done in different ways depending on the type of waste. Each method helps protect our planet by turning old materials into something useful again. Here are the main types:

1. Material Recycling

This type uses machines to break down old items like paper, plastic, or rubber into new products. For example, old car tires can be turned into playground surfaces.

2. Chemical Recycling

This process uses chemical reactions to change plastic waste into new raw materials. It’s a good way to recycle things that can’t be handled by machines alone.

3. Thermal Recycling

Also called energy recycling, this method burns waste to produce energy. It’s mostly used in factories to create electricity or heat.

4. Organic Recycling

This type is for natural waste like food scraps and leaves. It breaks down with the help of tiny organisms and becomes compost or gas that can be reused.
